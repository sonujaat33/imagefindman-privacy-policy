Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

ImageFindMan respects your privacy. This app allows users to search, preview, download, share, and manage publicly available images.

1. Information We Collect

  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not directly collect personal details such as your name, phone number, or email address inside the app.
  • When you search for images, your search query may be sent to third-party image/search services used by the app in order to fetch results.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Search history may be stored locally on your device if Search History is enabled.
  • Downloaded images are saved to your device gallery/storage when you choose to download them.
  • You can clear saved search history anytime from Settings.

3. Third-Party Services Used

This app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Serper / Google Images related service
  • Google Suggest service
  • Pexels API
  • Pixabay API

These services may process technical information such as device/app information, crash logs, ad-related data, or search requests as required for their functionality.

4. Ads

  • This app may display banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.
  • Ad providers may use device-related information to serve and measure ads according to their own policies.

5. Permissions

  • Internet: Required to search and load images and ads.
  • Media / Storage related access: Required to save downloaded images to your device and to share images.
  • Set Wallpaper: Required only when you choose to set an image as wallpaper.
  • Notifications: May be used to show image download notifications.

6. Search History

  • Search history is stored locally on your device, not on our own server.
  • You can disable search history from Settings.
  • You can also delete all saved search history from Settings.

7. Downloads and Sharing

  • When you download an image, it is saved to your device storage/gallery.
  • When you share an image, the app may temporarily create a shareable file in cache storage for sharing purposes.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, third-party services integrated into the app may process limited technical or usage data as part of providing ads, analytics, crash reporting, and search functionality.

9. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected by changing the “Last updated” date shown above.
